# GymLog

> *Track smarter. Lift harder. See the results.*

GymLog is a clean, focused iOS app built for lifters who want to spend less time fumbling with their phone and more time under the bar. Whether you're chasing a new PR or just staying consistent, GymLog keeps everything you need — right where you need it.

---

## Features

**Workout & Rest Timers**
Built-in timers keep your session on track. Start your workout timer when you begin, and let the rest timer count down your breaks so you stay focused and don't lose your tempo.

**Personal Best Tracking**
Visualize your progress over time with graphic views of your personal bests. Watch your numbers climb and stay motivated by seeing exactly how far you've come.

**Exercise Library**
Browse a comprehensive list of exercises across all muscle groups. Never stare at a blank screen wondering what to do next — just pick a movement and get to work.

---

## Getting Started

1. Clone the repo
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/yourusername/gymlog.git
   ```
2. Open `GymLog.xcodeproj` in Xcode
3. Build and run on your device or simulator (iOS 16+)

---

## Requirements

- iOS 16.0+
- Xcode 14+
- Swift 5.7+
